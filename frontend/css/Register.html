Hyperpoort message portal - Register

On this page you register yourself using the QR-code which provide to you. This QR-code you can scan on your phone with your mobile KVK ID app. If you give permission you will receive a 'Business Card'. With this 'Business Card' you authenticate yourself and submit your message on the Hyperportal.
