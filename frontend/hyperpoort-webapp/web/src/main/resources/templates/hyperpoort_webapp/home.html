Hyperpoort message portal - home

Hyperport is a shared service center where message traffic is processed for the government. Governments can use Hyperport to automate business processes and reporting chain processes. With Hyperport you can automate important steps in the processing proces of big amounts of (tax) declarations, business reports and notifications.


First you can register at Hyperport to get a 'Business Card'. You can then download the 'Business Card', or if you already have one, and submit directly onto Hyperport.
